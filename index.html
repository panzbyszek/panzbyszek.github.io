SAP BW Architect

Since January 2019, I have been serving as a SAP BW Architect and Team Leader at Orange Polska S.A. In my role, I successfully took over the SAP BW 7.5 environment following a comprehensive two-year implementation of the SAP system into Orange Polska S.A.

As a Team Leader, I manage a team of three internal BW developers and oversee a team of vendor BW consultants. My duties in this capacity span end-to-end report development using BW Mixed Scenarios (including aDSO, Open ODS Views, and Composite Providers).

In addition to this, I am responsible for the overall SAP BW system supervision and management. This includes job monitoring and scheduling, Process Chains and Data Flows remodeling, performance tuning, troubleshooting, and user role management.Here is a brief summary of my qualifications and experience.

My vast experience and dynamic leadership skills have significantly contributed to the successful implementation and management of SAP BW systems in Orange Polska S.A., ensuring efficient and effective operations.
